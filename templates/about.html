about me

Hello friends, I'm aayush. I like to write. I write to understand. This blog is a place where I document what I learn — mostly tech, sometimes life.

Intent?

My intent was to make a place for people to look at what i learn. By no means, is this website supposed to be my "portfolio page". You can check out my github if you want to have a look. This is just for my reflections.

I'm interested in machine learning, backend development, math, stats and everything geek. I like things simple, quiet, and focused.

Outside of code, I listen to hip-hop, read, and run long distances.

This blog is plain by choice — no fluff, just content (and also because it was easier to design and i don't really like designing. simple, sweet). If you learn something from it, good.

I hope this page is the only page that you hear a lot of 'i's from. Other pages are hopefully more knowledgeable and correct.
